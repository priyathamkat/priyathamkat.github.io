About

I am a 3rd year PhD student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Maryland, College Park, where I work with Prof. Soheil Feizi.

My primary research interests are in distributional robustness and multimodal learning. But, I love working on generalized algorithms that can solve a wide variety of problems.
