About

I am the Technical Lead at RELAI, where I lead the development and deployment of our AI solutions.

In 2024, I completed my PhD in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Maryland, College Park, where I worked with Prof. Soheil Feizi.

My research interests are in reliability of AI models and systems (including LLMs, AI agents). I love working on generalized algorithms that can solve a wide variety of problems.
